Basic Operations

The Prophecy Foundations disc is a multimedia player that organizes video, audio, and text. The media items are grouped on the right by a lesson revolving around a specific topic or by type of Bible study. Click the button and all of the materials related to it will expand below. Click the item to view it. You can filter items by typing in the search box.

Software Requirements

Adobe Acrobat Player must be installed to view the written content. Please go to http://get.adobe.com/reader/ to get the latest Adobe Acrobat Player.
